⌂ | Course selector

This visualisation shows all courses at Bauhaus-Universität Weimar in the current semester.

Results

By selecting an attribute (click on the corresponding bar in the diagram), the associated courses are displayed. In this way, with the help of this visualisation, the courses offered can be filtered according to faculty, language, day and SWS.

Short tips for interpreting the diagram:

  • Hovering over a band displays the related attributes and the number of occurrences.
  • By clicking on a bar of an attribute, the courses related to this attribute are displayed. Any number of bars can be selected, which are then displayed in red. To cancel the selection, the selected bar must be clicked on again.
  • The filtered courses are displayed in a list below the visualisation. Clicking on the course title opens the corresponding Bison page. Clicking on the teaching person displays the person in the teacher network.
